Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilites.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears have a long life expectancy, often 15 to 18 years.
  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • A teddy bear is a popular and well-known toy.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Pandas can climb as high as 13,000 feet and are also very good swimmers.
  • Pandas are shy; they don't venture into areas where people live.

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicadas

  • Flying cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Flying cicadas have five eyes.
  • Flying cicadas can camouflage on an olive tree.
